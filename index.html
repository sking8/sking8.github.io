Fan Feng 冯梵

Fan Feng is a final year PhD student at Dartmouth College advised by Prof. Bo Zhu. She is actively looking for job opportunities. Her research interests include physics-based simulation and optimization with tailored geometric representations. This site shows her publications, projects and miscellaneous art works.

She recieved B.S. in Computer Science and B.A in Mathematics and minor in studio arts form University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, advised by prof. Henry Fuchs.

Email: fan.feng.gr@dartmouth.edu or fanfengng@yahoo.com
